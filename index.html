Privacy Policy of JT Analytics

Glen Joy & Justin Thoms (collectively referred to as JT Analytics) operate the Online Gender Study website, which enables the collection of a small amount of data for a study regarding the statistical correlation between user gender and the amount of likes one receives.

This page is used to inform you regarding our policies with the collection, use, and publication of any data we retrieve.

If you choose to allow us permission to perform this study, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation with this policy. The Personal Information that we collect are used for strictly statistical purposes. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Using the Facebook API, we will be collecting data including, but not limited to, user gender, user likes, user names, and user posts. We will not be analyzing users individually. All data is analyzed as a collective. No individual user information will ever be published or shared in any manner.

Log Data

We want to inform you that when you visit our website, we collect information that your browser sends to us that is called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser version, pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics. These will not be viewed or used in any way.

Security

We value your trust in allowing us to use your group’s personal information for this study, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.
